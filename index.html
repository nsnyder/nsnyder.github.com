Bio

From a young age, Nathan Abel Snyder has been both academically and athletically inclined. He has always enjoyed developing and tinkering with technologies of various kinds and complexities. He is always seeking to challenge himself and learn more about different emerging technologies. Throughout highschool and continuing into college, Nathan has pursued his passion for running both in cross country and track and field. He enjoys the challenge and adventure of living life off the beaten path.

Nathan is currently working as a computer programmer at SOLO Labs in Kutztown, Pennsylvania. He will return to Grove City College in the fall, where he will spend his senior year studying computer science and will graduate in 2015.
