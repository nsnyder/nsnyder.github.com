Biography

Since he was young, Nathan Abel Snyder has enjoyed the challenge and fun of tinkering with different creative technologies, from LEGO to home networking. He is always seeking to challenge himself and learn more about different emerging technologies. This led him to pursue computer science at Grove City College. Throughout high school, college, and beyond, Nathan has also pursued a passion for running, participating in both cross country and track. In October 2015, he will complete his first (official) half marathon. He has also discovered the wonders of dancing, and enjoys it whenever possible. He thrives on the unexpected and loves trying new things.

Nathan graduated from Grove City College for computer science in May 2015. He is currently working at TheRestaurantStore.com as a web developer. He loves exploring the potential of the world in front of him.
